Section 1: Employee Handbook Overview
===========================================

Welcome to TechCorp Inc. This handbook outlines our company policies, procedures, and guidelines. All employees are expected to read and understand these policies upon joining the organization.

Our mission is to build innovative technology solutions that empower businesses worldwide. We value integrity, collaboration, innovation, and excellence in everything we do.

Section 2: Paid Time Off (PTO) Policy
===========================================

All full-time employees receive the following PTO benefits:
- 20 days of paid vacation per year (accrued monthly)
- 10 paid sick days per year
- 5 personal days per year
- 12 company holidays per year

PTO Carry-Over Policy:
Unused vacation days can be carried over to the next year, with a maximum of 5 days. Sick days do not carry over. Personal days do not carry over.

Requesting Time Off:
- Submit PTO requests through the HR portal at least 2 weeks in advance for vacations of 3+ days
- Sick leave requires notification to your manager before your shift starts
- Manager approval is required for all PTO except sick leave

Section 3: Remote Work Policy
===========================================

TechCorp supports a hybrid work model:
- Employees can work remotely up to 3 days per week
- Core office hours are Tuesday and Thursday (10 AM - 4 PM)
- Remote work from outside your home country requires VP approval
- Reliable internet connection (minimum 50 Mbps) is required
- Company will provide a $500 annual home office stipend

Section 4: Performance Review Process
===========================================

Performance reviews are conducted quarterly:
- Q1 Review (January): Goal setting and alignment
- Q2 Review (April): Mid-year check-in and feedback
- Q3 Review (July): Progress assessment
- Q4 Review (October): Annual performance evaluation

Rating Scale:
1 - Below Expectations
2 - Partially Meets Expectations
3 - Meets Expectations
4 - Exceeds Expectations
5 - Exceptional Performance

Promotion Criteria:
- Minimum rating of 4 for two consecutive quarters
- Demonstrated leadership and mentorship
- Technical or domain expertise growth
- Manager and skip-level manager recommendation

Section 5: Engineering Standards
===========================================

Code Review Policy:
- All code changes require a minimum of 2 peer approvals
- Reviews should be completed within 24 business hours
- Use conventional commit messages
- No self-approvals allowed

Testing Requirements:
- Minimum 80% code coverage for all services
- Unit tests required for all business logic
- Integration tests for API endpoints
- E2E tests for critical user flows

Deployment Process:
- All deployments go through CI/CD pipeline
- Blue-green deployment strategy for zero downtime
- Automated rollback on failure
- Production deployments only on weekdays (9 AM - 3 PM)
- Change freeze during company events and holidays

Section 6: Security Policies
===========================================

Access Control:
- Multi-factor authentication (MFA) required for all systems
- Password minimum: 12 characters, mixed case, numbers, symbols
- Password rotation: Every 90 days
- Principle of least privilege for all access grants

Data Classification:
- Public: Marketing materials, blog posts
- Internal: Internal wikis, Slack messages
- Confidential: Customer data, financial records
- Restricted: PII, credentials, security configurations

Incident Response:
- Severity 1 (Critical): Page on-call immediately, war room within 15 minutes
- Severity 2 (High): Response within 1 hour, team lead notification
- Severity 3 (Medium): Response within 4 hours, tracked in Jira
- Severity 4 (Low): Response within 24 hours, backlog item

Section 7: Benefits Summary
===========================================

Health Insurance:
- Company covers 90% of premium for employees
- Company covers 75% of premium for dependents
- Dental and vision included
- HSA contribution: $1,500/year from company

401(k) Retirement:
- Company matches 100% up to 4% of salary
- Immediate vesting
- Traditional and Roth options available

Professional Development:
- $3,000 annual learning budget per employee
- Conference attendance: up to 2 per year
- Certification reimbursement for relevant certifications
- Internal tech talks every Friday at 4 PM

Section 8: Onboarding Process
===========================================

Week 1:
- Day 1: IT setup, HR orientation, team introduction
- Day 2: Company culture and values workshop
- Day 3: Technical environment setup and access provisioning
- Day 4: Team-specific onboarding and project overview
- Day 5: First 30-60-90 day plan creation with manager

Week 2-4:
- Shadow team members on current projects
- Complete required compliance training
- Set up 1-on-1 meetings with key stakeholders
- Submit first code review (engineering) or first deliverable
- Complete onboarding checklist in HR portal

Buddy System:
Each new hire is assigned an onboarding buddy from their team who serves as the primary point of contact for the first 90 days.
